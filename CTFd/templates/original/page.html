Here Be Rules

  1. Team
    • 5 people per Team no more.
  2. Cooperation
    • There should be no cooperation between teams. Sharing flags or giving away hints is considered cheating.
    • Teams are not allowed to give themselves or any other team an unfair advantage
  3. ScoreBoard
    • Do not attack the infrastructure. Do not scan or attack the scoreboard server, IRC server, or any other RIT or CTF infrastructure.
  4. Sabotage
    • Do not attack other competing teams.
  5. Bruteforcing
    • Do not guess or bruteforce flags or answers.
  6. Denial of Service
    • No challenges call for Denial of Service attacks, and any DOS attacks are not allowed.
  7. Challenges
    • SSH is off limits, unless specifically stated for a challenge.
      • If we see SSH activity we will Ban the IP and if possible the team.
    • Web challenges will state the IP and port that challenge is on.
    • Reversing challenges will state the IP and port that challenge is on.
    • If any team, by any means, is able to get root access or anyt type of advantage, find the flag and leave the system alone. Do not ruin the competition for other people.
  8. Contact Us
      E-mail: rc3admin@rc3.club
